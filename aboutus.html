Endju?

Endju is a tech start-up venture based in Luanda, Angola, founded and registered in 2015. We commits and pull in different branches, being web and mobile solutions development the main ones.

We think business first, but we cannot advance if people around us are suffering and in constant need. That is why we cannot neglect the social entrepreneurship sector. We advocate for accountability, better education policies and community development practices, having local religious communities, government authorities and civil society as our main partners.

This predicament also extends to education, communities development and leadership. Africa has been out concern for long. By using technology we want to educate better in order to create less dependent communities. We got projects that encompass e-learning systems to train people in different ways of establishing a business , leadership skills, moral values, small scientific training and gadgets (do it yourself things) to help in agriculture (watering , climate changes, fertilization), water purification and supply, disease prevention, environment preservation etc.

What are our values?

Vision

Creating a dynamic ecosystem where communities and organizations innovate and benefit from rational use of technology in order to build a better society for the well-being of the society.

Mission

Lays on building products and solutions that: (a)- Bring the distance between commercial organization, public institutions and their shareholders to zero, therefore improving their relation and escalate.
(b)- Prepare youth to easily adapt and deal with the constant changing world of today, weather in job access, family or any other field.

Target

    Businesses, African communities (women, children), minorities (albinos, certain ethnic groups in danger of extensions) etc.

Philosophy

  • Innovate
  • Care more about others
  • Connect and reach out more
  • Respect for differences/diversities
  • Make money
